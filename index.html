Dear Diary...

My Journey as a Designer

A trial & error process

Like many designers, my love for creation started early. I realized sketching wasn't my strongest suit, but structural creation and problem-solving were. I wanted my creative output to live, breathe, and help people.

When faced with choices in high school, everyone pointed towards traditional directions. Instead, I spent weeks researching pathways where logic meets visuals. That's how I stumbled into the universe of **UI/UX Design**.

It wasn't a choice made because it sounded cool. It was a calculated journey of test courses, studying Google Material Design systems, analyzing micro-interactions on popular apps, and understanding how a change in button position can alter business conversion metrics.
